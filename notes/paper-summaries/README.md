# Paper Summaries

This directory contains concise summaries of research papers for quick reference.

## Purpose

These summaries are **condensed versions** for quick access and integration into the AI Brain architecture. For detailed reviews and analyses, see `/papers/`.

## What Goes Here

- Key takeaways (bullet points)
- Architecture implications
- Experiment ideas
- Quick reference notes

## What Goes in `/papers/`

- Detailed reviews
- Full analyses
- Complete writeups
- PDF references

## Template

Use [paper-summary-template.md](../../templates/paper-summary-template.md) but keep it concise.

## Naming Convention

Use format: `[first-author-lastname]-[year]-[short-title].md` or `[paper-short-title].md`

Example: `vaswani-2017-attention.md` or `attention-is-all-you-need.md`
